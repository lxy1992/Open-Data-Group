About HealthCal

Our Goal

Notify users how much calories they have taken in and tell users the time they needed to burn calories.

Our target users are pepole who want to keep fit, lose weight or keep slim. The goal of our project is to provie users a mordern and useful tool to calculate the amount of calories they have taked in and indicate the time they need to burn the calories.
