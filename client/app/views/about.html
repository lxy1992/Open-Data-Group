About HealthCal

Our Goal

To help users to control calories they take and maintain their health in an easy way

The goal of our project is to provie users a mordern and useful tool to identify the amount of calories they take in a day and the time they need to burn the calories. Firstly, users need to create their food lists with the calories they take. Then, users need to enter sports or activities and their weights. At last, the website will suggest the time they need to burn the calories accordingly.
